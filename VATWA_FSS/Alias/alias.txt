.dci .msg $aircraft Hello, it seems like you have taken a direct to CI $arrrwy. This is incorrect. When you're being radar vectored to final, you have to follow ATC instructions and maintain the intercept heading until your aircraft captures the localizer. So next time when a controller gives you a heading and clears you for the ILS approach, just put the heading in the Autopilot MCP and press the APP (or APPR) button and the aircraft will eventually intercept the localizer on its own. Taking a direct to CI $arrrwy messes up the approach and will NOT make you intercept the ILS properly. You can take direct FI $arrrwy AFTER you're established on LOC/ILS.
